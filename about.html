About

Ever since I was young, I've had an affinity for art, finding joy and expression through creativity. My chosen medium has been graphite drawings, where I've honed my skills and developed my unique style. I have mainly focused on pet portraits as of late, using colored pencils to capture the essence and personality of each subject. Recently, I've been exploring other mediums such as charcoal, oil pastels, and acrylic painting, and expanding into new art themes like landscapes and abstract art. As an up-and-coming artist, I continue to work diligently on my art, hoping to reach and inspire diverse audiences with my evolving creations.



- Sucely Duran Samaniego

My favorite piece is the Ocean. It is my favorite because it is the first time creating a piece at that size. I am happy with the end result and love the gradient of the piece.

My favorite medium to work with so far is Acrylics. I especially like how I can mix the colors, despite still experimenting with the medium I enjoy the mistakes I can make and cover them up with another layer of paint.

I usually start with a rough sketch and work from there. It takes a couple of tries before I am happy with the sketch, so I am constantly redoing my lines before I am adding my details. I try to have fun when making art and not make it perfect because I am still experimenting with different mediums.
